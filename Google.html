Summary: Predicted stock vs actual (select companies)

Google's twitter sentiment, google search volume, and retweet count were used as features to predict their closing stock price for each day in November/December 2019. The model had a r^2 value of .3..

We beleive this model was poor due to Google's search volume being related to search engine use, and not about information about the company.
